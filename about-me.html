About Me

I was born in the idyllic state of Kerala in India. I started my schooling in Kendriya Vidyalaya and moved across different states in the country with my parents, both managers in public sector companies. Then we moved to Jersey, UK where I finished my schooling from Le Rocquier School and Hautlieu School. After school, I went back to India to my home state to do my undergraduate degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Federal Institute of Science and Technology under Mahatma Gandhi University.

During my final year of Engineering I got campus placement with TCS and Standard Chartered GBS. After graduation, I took up the offer of Standard Chartered as Analyst. I worked there for a year till I called it quits in July 2017 to pursue my real passion in the fields of Data Science and Machine Learning. Now I am doing my Masters in Data Sciences at The University of British Columbia, Vancouver. I hope to become a Data Scientist, and a good one at that.

I also have interests like Web Development, Artificial Intelligence and Robotics. I try to widen my knowledge and skills by creating websites and doing various projects. Where my learning and online browsing fall short, I go for online courses and studies to help me with my projects. You are welcome find details of some of the stuff I have done in other pages.

I am a huge movie buff and a hardcore Chelsea fan. My weekends are spent catching up on football reruns when I can. I was part of the St. Clements F.C., Jersey in 2010. I also played as forward for the FISAT college football team while doing bachelors. Besides football, like every Indian, cricket is a big time fascination. I also like playing tennis in late evenings. In additions to watching movies, I listen to music. I used to play guitar and hope to get started on it again. I have tried my luck learning dancing and drums. I am a foodie, but only the eating part though I can cook for survival.
